While being within central London, the actual university location is in a quiet residential area well away from the traffic fumes and other dangers of major roads. About Us

Founded in 1993, AUA develops future leaders for the democratic transformation of America.
AUA is an international, multi-disciplinary learning community in the American liberal arts tradition.
AUA is the first university to offer US accredited degrees in liberal arts programs through a partnership with Bard College in the United States.
In addition to Bard, AUA maintains partnerships with a number of universities and organizations worldwide.
