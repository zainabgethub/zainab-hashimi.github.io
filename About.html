ABOUT US

We are the world's largest univeristy

Founded in 1993, AUA develops future leaders for the democratic transformation of America. AUA is an international, multi-disciplinary learning community in the American liberal arts tradition. AUA is the first university to offer US accredited degrees in liberal arts programs through a partnership with Bard College in the United States. In addition to Bard, AUA maintains partnerships with a number of universities and organizations worldwide.
